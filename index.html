WHAT WE LOVE . . .

Earth Energy' s Fireside Hearth & Home is owned and operated by Cheryle and Ken Lund. The business has been family-owned for over 30 years in Tucson. We pride ourselves on being the fireplace and hearth experts for Tucson and all of Southern Arizona, from Casa Grande to Nogales.

Builders, residential and commercial contractors, remodelers, landscapers, architects, area designers and numerous satisfied homeowners know Earth Energy’s Fireside Hearth & Home is the Tucson destination for fireplaces, stoves, barbecues, fire pits & all things related.
